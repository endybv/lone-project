Monticelli brusati

Due to its peculiar geographical position, on the hills of Franciacorta, the territory of Monticelli Brusati is part of both the Franciacorta Consortium and the mountain community of Sebino Bresciano.

Tourist Attraction

  • Tenuta La Montina

    The La Montina estates are located in Monticelli Brusati (Bs), in the extreme north-eastern part of Franciacorta, close to a large morainic amphitheater, where the carriage roads end and the wooded Valle Mugnina begins.

  • Monticelli Brusati waterfalls

    In the Gaina Valley in Monticelli Brusati the Path develops of the Falls, a path naturalistic ring of particular interest, characterized by the presence of a small canyon worked from the erosion of the stream that flows into the area and from a series of small waterfalls.
